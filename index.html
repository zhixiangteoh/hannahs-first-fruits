Our story

The first & best of the harvest

In the Bible, first fruits are the very first, best portion of the harvest — set apart and offered to God in gratitude before anything else was taken for oneself. An act of trust: giving the first and best, because He deserves it and is the giver of it all.

Hannah's First Fruits was born out of that same spirit. It started small — batches of fruit ice cream made in a home kitchen for friends — and has grown into an offering: real, whole ingredients, no shortcuts, and a lot of care in every batch.

Each flavour starts with fruit at its best: ripe, simple, unmasked by anything artificial. No dairy, no eggs — just pure, healthy ingredients, done well. Made by hand, one batch at a time. Thank you for being part of it.

Good to know

Frequently asked

How far in advance do I need to order?

At least 48 hours (2 days) before your preferred collection date. Every order is freshly made, so Hannah needs a little lead time to prepare.

Do you deliver?

Self-collection only, in the Bukit Panjang / Cashew area. If you'd like to arrange your own courier pick-up (e.g. Grab Courier), message Hannah to arrange.

What payment methods do you accept?

PayNow / PayLah only. At the point of order you'll receive a QR code with your order total.

What if my preferred time slot isn't available?

Slots are limited to keep every batch fresh. Please pick the next available slot, or message Hannah on Instagram / WhatsApp to check if it can be accommodated.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Cancellations more than 24 hours before your slot get a full refund. Flavour/size changes may be possible if requested before Hannah starts preparing — message as early as you can.

What if I can't collect my order?

One-time rescheduling is allowed for self-collection — just let Hannah know at least 24 hours in advance.

Is everything vegan?

Yes — all flavours are 100% plant-based, made without dairy or eggs.

Ready for this week's batch?

Order at least 48 hours ahead — tubs are limited to 15 a week.
